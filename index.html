Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 29, 2026

Privacy-First Design: This is a completely offline, local-only app. All your data stays on your device. No servers, no cloud, no internet connection required.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Snake! This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data.

Our app is a fully offline, ad-free casual game. All data is stored locally on your device - nothing is ever sent to any server.

2. Information We Collect

We do NOT collect any personal information.

The app may store the following locally on your device:

  • Local Game Data: Game scores and high score records stored in your device's local storage
  • App Preferences: Simple settings like language preference

3. How We Use Your Information

No information is collected or transmitted.

Any data stored locally is used only to:

  • Display your game progress and high scores within the app
  • Remember your language preference

4. Data Storage

All data stays on your device.

  • All game data is stored locally using Android's built-in storage
  • No data is ever sent to the cloud or any remote server
  • No internet or network connection is required to play
  • Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all data

5. Data Sharing

We do NOT share any data because we don't collect any.

This app:

  • Does NOT use any third-party analytics
  • Does NOT use any advertising services
  • Does NOT require any internet permission
  • Does NOT connect to any server

6. Children's Privacy

This app is safe for children of all ages. We do not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13 years of age.

7. Your Rights

Since we don't collect any data, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. All game data remains on your device and can be removed by uninstalling the app.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
